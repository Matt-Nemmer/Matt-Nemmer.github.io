Biomapper

Biomapper is a mobile application that allows ecologists to view forest data they need on color-coded maps. As part of my undergraduate capstone project, I had the role of team lead and ensured that the project was a major success.

Land Vehicle Simulator

This online simulator can be used to aid in the designing of land-based vehicles. It primarily features tools for four-wheeled vehicles. I previously designed the app using Java. However, I created a web-app version because I wanted it to be more accessible to the public.

STICH Scholar

I worked as the robotics and drone specialist for Northern Arizona University's Center for Science, Teaching, and Learning's STEM Interaction and Communications Hub (STICH). As such, I created educational materials and supported robotics programs involving youth and underprivileged communities. More specifically, I created educational materials about Arduino microcontrollers, recruited and surveyed FIRST robotics competition volunteers and team coaches, as well as creating a product list and curriculum for young students living in rural areas and on Native American reservations.
